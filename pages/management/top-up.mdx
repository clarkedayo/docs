# Add money to your card

All deposits get mixed automatically on deposit with TeleCard Mixer. 
This is to ensure that the funds are clean and safe to use.

1. Open the Main Menu on the @TeleCard_Robot and press 'Deposit'
2. Select network.
3. Select coin.
4. Enter the desired amount. Please be aware that there is a $5 fee for top-ups with a value below $50.
5. Send exactly the same or more to the address given by the bot.
6. Your deposit will complete automatically and the card balance will get updated.

![Image](https://files-hosdt.vercel.app/topup.png)



> Allow up to 10 minutes for the funds to apply to your card.
